Space Ocean

A platformer and shooter game made for Tavern Game Jam during Spring 24' under my pseudonym "AABattery0o0".

Space Ocean was created during a week long game jam alongside two other collaborators. I worked on tilesheets, the fish and monster sprites, and some of the GDScript in Godot. My good friend who goes by "LuggageGoblin" online mostly worked on the GDScript but also did a lot of work on the lighting, the player character sprite, level design, and tilesheets. The last collaborator with the user "_sujet0" worked on all of the sound design for the game.

Technologies Used:

  • Godot
  • Aseprite (For Pixel Art)
  • GitHub

Features:

  • Platformer
  • Shooter
